
By . Associated Press . The WTA says Sabine Lisicki has hit the fastest record serve in the history of the women's tennis tour, reaching 131 mph (211 kph). Lisicki hit the serve at 5-5, 40-30 of a first set tiebreaker during her third round match against Polona Hercog at the Bank of the West Classic in Stanford. That's 1 mph faster than Kim Clijsters was clocked in 2009. The previous record holder is Venus Williams, who reached 130 mph (209 kph) in a tournament in Japan in 2008.
