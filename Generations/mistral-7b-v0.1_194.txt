I've created a single page website that displays information about my dog and includes a photo gallery of him, along with his name, breed, age, weight, and favorite activities. The site also provides links to resources for dog owners.

#### Live link to deployed application:
https://handsome-villani-8c4a9e.netlify.app/

#### Link to GitHub Repo:
https://github.com/Garrett5236/Puppy_Page.git

![screenshot of puppy page](assets/images/puppyPageScreenshot.png)

### Usage:
#### Navigation Bar:
The navigation bar is located at the top of the page and includes links to several resources for dog owners, including a link to purchase treats from an online store.

#### Dog Info Section:
This section includes information about my dog, such as his name, breed, age, weight, and favorite activities. It also provides a link to an article about grooming tips.

#### Photo Gallery:
The photo gallery displays a collection of images of my dog, showcasing different aspects of his life as a beloved pet.

### Credits:
- The navigation bar was inspired by the [Bootstrap Navbar example](https://getbootstrap.com/docs/5.1/examples/navbar/) on the Bootstrap website.

- The photo gallery was created using the [Gallery component](https://getbootstrap.com/docs/5.1/components/image-gallery/) from the Bootstrap documentation.
