Bayern Munich legend Franz Beckenbauer believes Jurgen Klopp could succeed Pep Guardiola at the Allianz Arena. Klopp, who won two Bundesliga titles in seven years, has confirmed he will leave Borussia Dortmund when his contract expires this summer.The former Liverpool boss, 50, has also been linked with Manchester City and Real Madrid but Beckenbauer believes Bayern could be a better fit for Klopp’s style of play. He told Sport Bild: “Klopp would definitely like to coach Bayern one day.” “I think he could bring Bayern back to the top level that they have had in recent years.“He has won everything there is to win at Dortmund and it will be hard to keep him there for long.”
