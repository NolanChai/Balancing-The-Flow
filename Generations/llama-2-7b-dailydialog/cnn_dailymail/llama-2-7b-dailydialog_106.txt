The boy's father had also been having an affair and had spent time in jail for frauding his own mother out of a million pounds. After finding out about her husbands double life the depressed mother took both her son and herself to London Bridge Station where she killed them both by jumping on the tracks.

### Assessment:

The case is an example of how one unfortunate event can trigger off another unfortunate event and so on and so forth, until a person finds it impossible to deal with their circumstances and ends up taking drastic measures.
