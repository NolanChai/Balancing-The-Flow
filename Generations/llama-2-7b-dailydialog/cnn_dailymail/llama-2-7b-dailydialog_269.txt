### Explanation:

The question is trying to get the interviewee's opinion about their willpower and determination in general. A candidate with strong resolve can make the choice to not click the button if they were instructed not to do so even though it would be easy for them to give in to temptation. The opposite of that would be a person who was weak willed and couldn't resist the temptation to press the button, even if doing so would have resulted in severe consequences or punishment.
