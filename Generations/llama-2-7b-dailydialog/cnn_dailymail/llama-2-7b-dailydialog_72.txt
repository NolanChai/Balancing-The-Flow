As if he were still there, the shaken officers told jurors that they could hear the gunman's voice shouting as they tried to help victims and locate suspect James Holmes during the chaotic minutes after the July 2012 attack on a midnight premiere of the Batman film "The Dark Knight Rises."

### Context:

Holmes, 35, is accused of opening fire in the packed auditorium, killing 12 people and wounding nearly 70. The jury heard from eight witnesses on the first day of Holmes' murder trial, including police officers who entered the theater.
