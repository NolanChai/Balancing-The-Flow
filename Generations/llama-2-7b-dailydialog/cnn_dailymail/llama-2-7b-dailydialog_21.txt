A cleaner who feared a body he found was a murder victim had in fact found a 900-year-old mummy. The preserved remains were discovered in a cardboard box by street cleaners in north-western Peru. The mummified woman was found with her arms wrapped around her chest and her hair tied into a bun. It is believed the woman died from natural causes.
