Most cases of infertility are caused by women. But, some men may also suffer from infertility problem. According to the survey of fertility center in 1995, about 30% of infertile couples has problems related to man and it was 70% in women. The reasons for male infertility are mostly found in sperm cells which have decreased in number or quality or both. Some men also suffer from low hormone level due to genetics, environmental condition or lifestyle. In addition, some medications used in the treatment of man can lower fertility in some people.
