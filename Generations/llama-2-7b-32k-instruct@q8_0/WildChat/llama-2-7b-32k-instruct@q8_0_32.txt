Mission Title: Respectful Men

Mission Type: Story Mission

Description:  You will start this mission from your apartment. CJ goes to visit his parents and then they tell him about the problems with Ryder. They need help dealing with their son as he has become a bit of an outcast in town, but CJ can't leave them to deal with it alone. He says that he will do what he can and sets off for his next destination.

Mission Goal:  Meet up with Ryder's parents and help them work out their son's problems.

Objectives:  Complete a mission called "Respectful Men" in order to unlock this mission.

Difficulty Level: 2/5

Reward:  A new car, the Comet.

Completion Percentage: 100%
