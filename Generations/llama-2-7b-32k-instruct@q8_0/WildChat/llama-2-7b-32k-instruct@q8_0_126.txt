Client Brief: Neli Arah's Interactive Flexi Foodie Product 

Objective: To create an interactive digital media product for Neli Arah's Food vlog that caters to teenagers adopting a flexitarian diet. The product should be accessible on various devices, including smartphones and tablets, with sections organized by four diet types: meat-based, pescatarian, vegetarian, and vegan. It should include information about the flexitarian lifestyle, at least one recipe for each diet type, audio/video instructions for some recipies, and be detailed enough to demonstrate your interactive digital media product creation skills.

Target Audience: The target audience is teenagers who are adopting a flexitarian diet or interested in exploring it further. They should be aged between 16-25 and have access to smartphones, tablets, or similar devices.

Key Messages: The key messages should focus on promoting the benefits of the flexitarian lifestyle, highlighting the range of recipes available for each diet type, and showcasing the interactive features of the product.

Deliverables: The deliverables include a detailed outline of the content that will be included in the interactive digital media product, as well as wireframes or prototypes demonstrating how it will function on various devices.

Timeline: The project is expected to take approximately 4-6 weeks.

Budget: A budget of £2000 has been allocated for this project.
