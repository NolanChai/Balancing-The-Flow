August 26 is National Dog Day
=============================

Hey there, fellow dog lovers! Are you ready to celebrate a very special day? It's time to commemorate all of our furry four-legged friends on **National Dog Day**. This special holiday was created in 2013 by Colleen Paige, a pet lifestyle expert and animal advocate, to raise awareness about the importance of adopting dogs from shelters and rescues.

On National Dog Day, we encourage everyone to take action in one or more of the following ways:
