Smita Srivastava holds the Guinness record for the longest braid - which measures 79 feet, 2 inches - and is currently raising money to buy a plane ticket that would take her from India to London. Her ultimate goal: setting the world record for the world's longest hair. (Source: AOL News)

The woman who holds the world record for the longest braid in the world hopes to one day break the world record for the world's longest hair.
