Bio: Christopher Charles "Chris" Chase (born April 12, 1967) is an American journalist . He began his career with NBC Sports and CBS Sports before moving to CNN where he spent much of his time covering boxing in the 1980s and early 90's. Since then Chris has worked for ESPN and MSNBC, but most recently gained prominence at Fox News Channel when he served as a reporter during the coverage of Hurricane Katrina . Chase was also one of three anchors on the show Red Eye with Greg Gutfeld from 2007 to 2013."
