Deepwater Horizon oil spill: Five years later 
(CNN)Five years after the Deepwater Horizon rig exploded and unleashed the largest marine oil spill in the nation's history, we are still experiencing -- yet only beginning to truly understand -- its profound environmental and economic repercussions.

The impact of the spill on local communities has been felt both onshore and off; in the Gulf and across the country. Here are a few ways that Deepwater Horizon's legacy lives on, five years later.
