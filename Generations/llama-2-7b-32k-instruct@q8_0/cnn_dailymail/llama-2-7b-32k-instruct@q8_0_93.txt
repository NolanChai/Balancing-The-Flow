Title: Rafa Nadal gets clay court season off to perfect start with Monte Carlo Masters victory

Rafael Nadal has gotten his clay court season off to a perfect, confidence-boosting start, beating Lucas Pouille 6-2, 6-1 at the Monte Carlo Masters. The Spanish tennis star was dominant throughout the match and will now be confident of retaining his title in Rome on Sunday. He'll play Dominic Thiem next.
