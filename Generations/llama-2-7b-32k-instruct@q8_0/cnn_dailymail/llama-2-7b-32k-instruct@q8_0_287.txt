This article is about how an American company named Constellation Group recently bought a majority share of the hotel group that includes Claridges, the Connaught, and the Berkeley in London. This deal has caused quite a stir among people interested in luxury hotels. The article mentions some of the reasons for the purchase, including the fact that the hotels are located in prime locations and have a rich history. It also notes that Constellation Group is planning to make some changes to improve the quality of service and amenities at these hotels.
