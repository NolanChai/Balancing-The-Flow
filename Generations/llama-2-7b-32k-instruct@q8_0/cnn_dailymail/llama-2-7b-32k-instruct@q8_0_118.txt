Lewis Hamilton has won his home British Grand Prix at Silverstone to extend his Formula One title lead over Sebastian Vettel to 20 points. Hamilton drove a nearly flawless race to take the checkered flag first after making his final pit stop with just eight laps remaining Sunday. The two-time F1 champion finished ahead of Vettel and Ferrari teammate Kimi Raikkonen, giving him a total of six wins this season for Mercedes . Hamilton now leads the German driver 205 points to 183 points after nine races . "The weekend started off bad and it finished perfect," he said. "We were fighting for our lives."
