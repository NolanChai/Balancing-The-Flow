Mahendra Ahirwar's parents admit they would rather their son die than keep suffering   The boy, who suffers from a condition that causes his head to tilt 180 degrees, is one of the world's rarest congenital deformities. His father Mahendra, 54, said: "If my son dies, it would be like heaven for us."   The Ahirwar family have been shunned by their community in the central Indian town of Barwani and often face ridicule from passers-by because of their son's condition.
