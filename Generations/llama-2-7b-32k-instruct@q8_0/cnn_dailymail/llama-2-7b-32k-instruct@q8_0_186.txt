The show is filmed mainly in and around London, England . It began on 4 October 2011 and has since aired four series. It was initially broadcast as two programmes each week; "Made In Chelsea" at 9pm and the follow-up programme Made In Chelsea After Hours which was shown at 10pm. However, in Series 3 this changed to just one programme per week at 8: 30 pm. The show is known for its glitzy lifestyle, designer clothes and outrageous parties . It also covers the ups and downs of friendships, relationships and family life.
