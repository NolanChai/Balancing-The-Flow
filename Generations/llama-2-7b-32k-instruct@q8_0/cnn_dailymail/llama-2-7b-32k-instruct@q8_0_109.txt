Floyd Mayweather Jr . will defend his World Boxing Association (WBA) welterweight title against Argentine challenger Marcos Maidana on September 14 at the MGM Grand Arena in Las Vegas, Nevada. The bout is being promoted as a clash of styles between two of the best boxers in the world. Mayweather Jr. (47-0, 26 knockouts) is known for his defensive skills and footwork, while Maidana (35-4, 18 KOs) has an aggressive style that often leads him to victory over lesser opponents. The fight is scheduled to start at 9 pm EDT (0200 GMT).
