Dortmund Striker Targetted by Liverpool (Sports World News)Liverpool are showing interest in Borussia Dortmund striker Ciro Immobile. The Italy international has not had the best of times in the Bundesliga following his move from Torino last summer and Dortmund have since been keen on re-signing their former player Robert Lewandowski who departed to Bayern Munich a year ago.
