The incident occurred at around 8 p.m. local time on Thursday near the city's famed Golden Gate Bridge, where officers had been responding to reports of a person carrying a knife . The San Francisco Police Department confirmed that its officers attempted to stop two vehicles and identified them as a 2015 Nissan Altima and a 2016 Hyundai Accent. Officers were able to successfully stop the Nissan, but they did not catch the driver of the Hyundai . After fleeing officers for several minutes, the vehicle collided with the two victims .
