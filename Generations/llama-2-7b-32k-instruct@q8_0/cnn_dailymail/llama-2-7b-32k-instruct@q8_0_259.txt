The 24-year-old has been plagued by injuries since joining Madrid in a world record deal from Tottenham Hotspur in September 2013, but was back to his best last season as he scored nine goals and provided 16 assists in all competitions . Bale also led Wales to the semi-finals of Euro 2016.
