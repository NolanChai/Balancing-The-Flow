Messi expected to return for Barcelona vs. Celta Vigo 
Lionel Messi has recovered from his injured foot and should be fit to start Sunday's La Liga match with Celta Vigo. The Argentina forward sat out both of his country's friendlies against El Salvador as a precautionary measure. He had been suffering from an issue in the foot that first showed up during the International Champions Cup, but has now made a full recovery. (Source: ESPN)
