A bus crashed into a passenger plane at an airport in Uzbekistan, leaving two people dead and six injured on Sunday, local news agency Atabek reported citing police. Video footage shows the driver of the bus walking out of the aircraft as firefighters battled to put the flames out. (At a glance: What you need to know about travel in Uzbekistan)

The incident is difficult to comprehend, as the collision was much more devastating than an aviation accident with the same impact on human life would seemingly be, experts told Reuters, citing similar instances of bus collisions into aircraft.
