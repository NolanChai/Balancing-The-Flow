England midfielders Jordan Henderson and Jack Wilshere are the surprise names on Manchester City's summer shopping list, according to reports in England on Friday .

The struggling Barclays Premier League champions know they need more English players in their team if they hope to challenge for the title next season. They also believe that Wilshere and Henderson could add an extra dimension to the way they play.

England coach Roy Hodgson was understood to have recommended both players to Manchester City manager Manuel Pellegrini, although it is believed that there has been no contact from either club yet .

City are also interested in bringing in Tottenham midfielder Eric Dier and Southampton defender Ryan Bertrand.
