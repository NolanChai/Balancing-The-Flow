Title: Pilot plunges jet into dive to save lives after cabin pressure loss
A SkyWest Airlines pilot plunged a plane carrying 75 passengers into a terrifying dive on Wednesday after a reported, potentially catastrophic loss of cabin pressure in mid-air. Frightened passengers were heard screaming as the Boeing 737 dropped thousands of feet in minutes and then quickly pulled out of the dive, landing safely at Salt Lake City International Airport about an hour later.
