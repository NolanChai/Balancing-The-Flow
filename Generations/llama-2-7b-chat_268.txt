(CNN) -- Thursday's draw for the last 16 of the European Champions League was filled with surprises, as several heavyweights were paired against each other in what promises to be an exciting round of matches. In addition to the highly anticipated matchup between Real Madrid and Manchester United, Barcelona will face off against Paris Saint-Germain, while Bayern Munich takes on Liverpool. The draw also threw up some intriguing matchups between teams that have never faced each other in the competition before, such as Roma against Ajax and Atletico Madrid against Juventus. With so many top-level teams involved, fans are sure to be treated to a thrilling round of matches that will determine who will advance to the quarterfinals.
