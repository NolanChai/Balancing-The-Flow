He tried to use the same phone charger which he used for charging his phone to start the engine of this car but the battery was not getting charged since its dead. In order to make the battery charge, he started using a different phone charger and connected it to the car battery through the socket. The flammable material inside the phone charger caught fire soon after which the entire hall got burned down.
### Explanation:
The phone charger which is used for charging your mobile phone contains the flammable material because the electronics in that phone gets damaged and so to replace them, they use those materials (flammable materials) inside these phones. So when he tried connecting a new phone charger to this car battery, it ignited since there was a short circuit between the two and caused the entire hall to catch fire.
### Solution:
This is an obvious one. Just don't do that!
