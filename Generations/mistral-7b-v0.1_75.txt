### Summary:
In 2018, the FDA issued a recall on certain antibiotic drugs for the possibility of tendon ruptures and tendinits due to severe side effects from the medication. The following table includes a list of all affected drugs and their manufacturer.

|Drug Name|Company Name|
|---|---|
|Fluoroquinolone Antibacterial Drug Products|Various|

### Notes:
* The FDA warning was issued for any Fluoroquinolone antibiotic drug products due to the increased risk of tendon ruptures and tendinits.
* The FDA has also advised patients, doctors, pharmacists and others who prescribe or dispense these drugs to be aware of this label change and discuss any risks with their doctor if they are currently taking these antibiotic drugs.

* Drug Name - The name of the affected drug
* Company Name - The company that manufactures the drug
