Ali-Mohamed Mohamud was convicted Thursday of second-degree murder for beating to death his 10-month-old daughter, Zainab, in their home in Maine. According to the police report, Mohamud had been babysitting Zainab when he became frustrated with her constant crying and hit her multiple times on the head with a blunt object, causing fatal injuries. The incident occurred on October 12, 2012, and Mohamud was arrested and charged with murder the following day. He was found guilty of second-degree murder after a trial that lasted several days.
