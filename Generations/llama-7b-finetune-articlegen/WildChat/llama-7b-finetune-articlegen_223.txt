1. ABM is an acronym that stands for account-based marketing.
2. Account-based marketing (ABM) focuses on high-value customers rather than the mass audience.
3. To implement an ABM strategy, a company needs to identify its target accounts.
4. The next step in creating and implementing an ABM strategy is to create personas.
5. It's important for marketers to get buy-in from sales.
6. A great way to start using account-based marketing is by having conversations with your customers.
7. You can use the buyer personas you created in step 2 of creating and implementing an ABM strategy.
