The former Scottish First Minister Alex Salmond once appeared in an obscure Pakistani soap opera, it emerged today. The actor starred alongside Bollywood legend Sunil Dutt and the famous Indian actor Shakila. His appearance on the show was widely criticised at the time but he went on to star as a major character in his own political career, serving for almost eight years between 2007 and 2014.
