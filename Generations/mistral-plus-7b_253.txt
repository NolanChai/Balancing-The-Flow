Since the infidelity scandal that took down the CIA chief David Petraeus broke last Friday, a scattered timeline of bizarre events have captivated, if not confused, the public. The FBI first began investigating Paula Broadwell, Petraeus’s biographer and mistress, after discovering she had hacked into another woman’s email account. During their investigation, they found emails between her and Petraeus that revealed an extramarital affair and prompted the resignation of the General.
