But little did anyone know that something much more sinister was brewing in the shadows. A new virus, unlike anything seen before, had emerged from the depths of the Amazon rainforest and was spreading rapidly across the globe. The virus, known as the ‘Amazon Flu’, was highly contagious and deadly, infecting millions of people within just a few weeks of its emergence. Governments scrambled to contain the outbreak, but it was too late. The Amazon Flu had already spread beyond control and was wreaking havoc on populations worldwide.
