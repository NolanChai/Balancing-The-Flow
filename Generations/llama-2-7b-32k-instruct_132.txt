A blast occurred at around 4pm local time on Monday (1400 GMT) and injured five people including three police officers. The incident took place in central Prague, near the US Embassy, just outside the Křižík Fountain. Firefighters said they were dealing with an "incendiary device" while the Interior Ministry identified it as a bomb. An official source confirmed that one of the injured victims was a police officer, while other officers and civilians suffered minor injuries.
