
1. By Ted Thornhill  2. Elections are sure to throw up eccentric characters from time to time with a colorful back story  – but none quite like the candidates running for mayor in one Colorado town. Voters will be asked to choose between a man who wants to legalize marijuana and a woman who claims she was abducted by aliens at the age of eight. The candidates in the small city of Woodland Park, just outside of Colorado Springs, have been dubbed ‘the pot head and the alien’.
