
Diane Keaton reiterated her support of Woody Allen in an interview with USA Today on August 19, 2020. She was asked to respond to the recent allegations of abuse made against him by his daughter Dylan Farrow. In a statement, she said, "Woody Allen has been my friend and colleague for almost thirty years. I have no reason not to believe him."

### Analysis:

Keaton's support of Woody Allen is a sign that she is unwilling to listen to the allegations made against him by his daughter Dylan Farrow. By reiterating her belief in him, she is showing solidarity with him and expressing her disbelief in the accusations against him. This indicates that Keaton has not been swayed by the recent media attention surrounding this controversy and remains loyal to Allen despite the allegations of abuse made against him.
