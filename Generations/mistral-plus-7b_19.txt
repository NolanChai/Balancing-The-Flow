Austin County Sheriff's Office deputies responded to a report of an injured man at a residence in rural west Houston Sunday afternoon, according to a news release. The man was found inside a home and taken by ambulance to a local hospital where he was pronounced dead.
The victim has been identified as Dr. Mark Hausknecht, 65, who was shot multiple times while riding his bicycle near Texas Medical Center, the sheriff's office said. He died at about 1:30 p.m. Sunday, according to Harris County Institute of Forensic Sciences.
Investigators believe that Hausknecht was the target of the shooting but have not determined a motive. The shooting is believed to be an isolated incident and there is no threat to public safety, authorities said.
