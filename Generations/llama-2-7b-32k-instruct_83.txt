There are three victims of the construction accident in New York. Two of them were employees of the company contracted to raze the building, and the other was someone who happened to be nearby . According to police, the accident occurred after a building being demolished collapsed on Wednesday morning and nearly obliterated an adjacent four-story townhome. The two people killed at the site were workers for the company hired to tear down the building, while a third person was killed when his or her vehicle struck one of the falling pieces of debris . A fourth victim was a woman who happened to be in an auto nearby and died after being struck by flying debris from the construction accident.
