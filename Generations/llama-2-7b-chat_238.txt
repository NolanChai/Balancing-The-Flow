According to the article, the photographs show how properties along the Thames have been damaged by river levels reaching a ten-year high on some stretches. The pictures from Surrey come as communities in the area are bracing themselves for more flooding due to heavy rainfall and high tides. The article goes on to say that the Environment Agency has warned of "significant" flooding along the Thames, with properties at risk of being submerged in the coming days. In addition, the agency has urged residents to prepare for the worst and move valuables to higher ground to avoid any damage. The article also mentions that the flooding is not just limited to Surrey, but is affecting other areas along the Thames, including Berkshire and Oxfordshire.
