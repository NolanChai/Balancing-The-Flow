Street performers, also known as buskers, are entertainers who perform in public spaces such as streets, sidewalks, and parks for donations of money. They use a variety of talents to entertain passersby, including music, dance, comedy, juggling, and magic. Street performers often set up in busy areas like tourist spots, shopping districts, or subway stations to attract an audience and solicit tips or donations. Busking is a popular form of street entertainment in many parts of the world.
