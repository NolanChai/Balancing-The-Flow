As the gang of thieves entered Selfridges department store in central London, they were met with confused glances but also an air of admiration from some shoppers. Dressed in full burkas, complete with designer headdresses and matching handbags, they cut a striking figure amidst the bustling crowds of Oxford Street. Little did anyone suspect that beneath their elaborate disguises lay a carefully orchestrated heist, with the watches at the center of their cunning plan.
