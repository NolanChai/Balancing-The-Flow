Dzeko has been linked with a move to AC Milan for several weeks, and sources close to the player have confirmed that he is eager to make the switch to the San Siro. Despite interest from other clubs, including Manchester United and Barcelona, Dzeko has made it clear that his priority is to join the Italian giants. "I've always been a fan of AC Milan and I think they are the best team in Italy," Dzeko told CNN. "I would be honored to play for them and help the team achieve success."
