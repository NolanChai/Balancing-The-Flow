 Wolfsburg striker Edin Dzeko has stated that he expects to play for AC Milan next season, according to reports from CNN. The 29-year-old, who is currently on loan from Roma at the German club, is out of contract with Wolfsburg at the end of this season and has been linked to a move to Italy. Dzeko scored 16 goals in all competitions for Wolfsburg this season, making him one of the top scorers in the Bundesliga.
