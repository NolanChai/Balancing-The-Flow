The CDC is still investigating whether these cases of acute flaccid myelitis (AFM) are part of an ongoing outbreak, as they first thought when a dozen or more children were sickened in 2014. Researchers at the agency say they can't rule out a connection between this year's AFM cases and those in 2014.
