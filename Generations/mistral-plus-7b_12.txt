The makers of Gorilla Glass, Corning Inc., have announced the release of their latest iteration of this popular protective material: Gorilla Glass 4. This new version is 20% thinner than its predecessor while still offering increased protection against scratches and cracks. The toughest Gorilla Glass ever created, Gorilla Glass 4 offers up to four times the damage resistance of competitive glass.

Gorilla Glass 4 has been designed for use in a wide range of touchscreen devices, including smartphones, tablets, wearables and other mobile devices. It is also being incorporated into some automotive displays as well as medical equipment. The new Gorilla Glass 4 offers improved scratch resistance and reduces the likelihood of breakage during drops or impacts.
