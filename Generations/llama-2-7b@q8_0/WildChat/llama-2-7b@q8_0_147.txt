* [Celestine Weeks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Celestine_Weeks) is a 29 year-old woman from Los Angeles who suffered severe injuries in an automobile accident when she was only 15 years old. The trauma caused severe brain damage and left her permanently incapacitated with quadriplegia, cerebral palsy, epilepsy, blindness, deafness, dysphagia, chronic pain, cognitive impairment, incontinence and ventilator dependency.
