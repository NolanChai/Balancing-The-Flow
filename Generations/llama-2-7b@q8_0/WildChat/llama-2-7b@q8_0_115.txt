Philo's Biblical Antiquities is a book that includes his interpretations on the Old Testament and also relates them with the ancient Jewish wisdom. Philo uses both Old Testaments in order to interpret each other, and he compares the texts and draws conclusions based on them.

The book "The Legend of the Jews" was a collection of folk-tales that were intended for Jewish children. The tales in the book are usually about miracles, prophecies, and other fantastical events that happened to the ancient Israelites. There are also some tales which talk about Jewish rituals such as circumcision.
