The new format allows users to see all tweets that are related to a topic -- not just those from accounts they follow. Users can also now see how many people are talking about a subject and what other tweets mention a specific word or phrase.
"We want to make it easy to see everything you need for any given moment, but not everything that's possible," Twitter engineer Adam Messinger wrote in a blog post Wednesday. "This is the first step on our journey towards building a more comprehensive timeline."
Users can customize their experience and set how many tweets they want to see when browsing topics, Messinger wrote. The feature will be available for mobile devices running iOS 6 or later, as well as Android 4.0 Ice Cream Sandwich or higher, he added.
