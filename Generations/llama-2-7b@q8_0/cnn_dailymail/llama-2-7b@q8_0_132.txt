A powerful explosion has damaged a building in the centre of the Czech capital, Prague, injuring up to eight people.
Firefighters are searching for possible casualties after the blast rocked an office block on Poupatele 3 street at around 5am (4pm BST) today.
The cause of the explosion is not yet clear but authorities have said it may be a gas leak.
'There was a strong explosion,' a police spokesperson told Czech Television, adding that the force of the blast blew out windows on the upper floors and sent glass flying onto the street below.
