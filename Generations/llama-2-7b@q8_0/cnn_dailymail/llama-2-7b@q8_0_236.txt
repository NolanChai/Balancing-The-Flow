“The ban will be from two games, so that’s a big blow for us,” Brondby coach Morten Wieghorst told Ekstra Bladet. “Agger is our most important player and we will miss him.”
The 21-year-old is in his second season with the Danish side after signing from Manchester United in July last year on a four-year contract which has an option for two further seasons.
