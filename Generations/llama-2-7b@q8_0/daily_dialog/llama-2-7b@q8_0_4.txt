How many steps are there in its stairway ?

 There are 1706 steps to reach the top . 

 What is it made of ?

 It's iron - a special kind of steel - and weighs 7,350 tons , which means it weighs as much as eight Eiffel Tower's on wheels! 

How can you get to the top?

 You can take an elevator ( lift ) or walk up the stairs . 

 How many people visit the tower every day?

 More than five million tourists go up it each year .
