The 2007 Virginia Tech Hokies football team was an American college football team that represented Virginia Tech, a public research university in Blacksburg, Virginia. Led by third-year head coach Frank Beamer and his 30th season as the head coach of the Hokies, the team played their home games at Lane Stadium and were members of the Atlantic Coast Conference (ACC). They finished the season 9–4, 5–2 in ACC play to finish in third place in the conference's Coastal Division. They received an invitation to the Gator Bowl where they lost to Auburn. The team was ranked #16 in the final AP Poll of the season.
