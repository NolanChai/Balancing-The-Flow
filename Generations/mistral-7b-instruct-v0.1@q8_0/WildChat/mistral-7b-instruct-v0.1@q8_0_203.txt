The upcoming Blue Beetle movie will utilize the concept of superhero legacies on film and could be included in the new DC Universe. In the comics, Jaime Reyes is the third Blue Beetle, but the movie's director has confirmed that there are others who have held the mantle as well. This legacy aspect will make Blue Beetle more relatable to audiences and set it apart from other superhero movies. The movie is set in the DC Extended Universe but could also be rebooted into the new DC Universe movies. With its focus on legacy, Blue Beetle has the potential to make superhero heritage a celebrated component in film genre.
