Client Brief: Neli Arah's Interactive Flexi Foodie Product

Background:
Neli Arah is a food vlogger that wants to extend their audience to attract teenagers who are adopting a flexitarian diet. They have commissioned you to create an interactive digital media product to help them achieve this goal. The interactive digital media product will be used on smartphones, tablets and similar devices. It will be organised into sections. One for each of four diet types: meat-based, pescatarian, vegetarian and vegan. Users will be able to navigate through the product and select content they want to access according to diet type.

Goals:
The primary goal of this interactive digital media product is to provide information about flexitarianism, as well as at least one recipe for each diet type included. Additionally, the product should include audio/video instructions for some of the recipes to make it more accessible and user-friendly.

Target Audience:
The target audience for this product is teenagers who are adopting a flexitarian diet. The product must be designed in a way that will appeal to this audience and provide them with the information and resources they need to maintain their dietary choices.

Content:
The interactive digital media product will consist of four sections, one for each of the following diet types: meat-based, pescatarian, vegetarian, and vegan. Each section will include the following elements:

1. Information about flexitarianism: This section will provide an overview of what a flexitarian diet is, its benefits, and why it may be a good choice for some people.
2. Recipes: The product will include at least one recipe for each diet type included. These recipes will be simple to make, healthy, and delicious. They should be accompanied by clear instructions and photographs to help users understand how to prepare the dish.
3. Audio/video instructions: For some of the recipes, audio/video instructions will be provided to demonstrate how to prepare the dish. This will make it easier for users who may not have experience in cooking to follow the recipe successfully.

Design and Usability:
The interactive digital media product will be designed to be user-friendly and accessible. It will include clear navigation, allowing users to easily select the diet type they are interested in. The content will be presented in an engaging and visually appealing way, using a combination of text, images, video, and sound to make it more engaging for the user.

Accessibility:
The interactive digital media product must be accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. It should be designed with accessibility in mind, and include features such as text-to-speech functionality, closed captioning, and high-contrast mode to make it easier for users to access the content.

Conclusion:
In conclusion, Neli Arah's Interactive Flexi Foodie Product is a great opportunity to showcase your interactive digital media product creation skills. The product should be designed to appeal to teenagers who are adopting a flexitarian diet, providing them with the information and resources they need to maintain their dietary choices. By including recipes, audio/video instructions, and other engaging content, the product will help Neli Arah attract a wider audience and increase their influence in the food industry.
