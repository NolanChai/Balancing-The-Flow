Cook made the switch to Moldova earlier this month after receiving an invitation from the country’s President Nicolae Timofti to represent the nation at the Olympics. He said: “I have had an opportunity to train with some of the best fighters in the world and I am now ready for a new challenge.”

The Brit has been training with Moldova’s national team in Turkey and was selected for the squad after impressing coaches. Cook added: “It is a huge honour to be representing Moldova at the Olympics, an opportunity that I would never have received if it wasn’t for the support and guidance of President Timofti.”

The 2016 Olympic Games in Rio de Janeiro will take place from August 5th – 21st.
