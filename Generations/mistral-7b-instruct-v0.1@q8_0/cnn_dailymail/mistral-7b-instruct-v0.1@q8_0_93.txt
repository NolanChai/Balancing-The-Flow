Nadal served exceptionally well, winning seven of his eight service games, and hit 24 winners past Pouille to take his tally for the year to six. The Spaniard is now 17-0 on clay, a run that began in Monte Carlo in 2005 and includes an unprecedented 10 French Open titles. "I played well," Nadal said.
