Police in North Carolina say a married couple is dead after an Amtrak train hit them on Easter morning as they lingered on the tracks. Derek Lowe, 38, and Tina Lowe, 33, were hit shortly before 10am outside of Charlotte. Police said the couple had been walking on the tracks when the train struck them. It is not clear why they were on the tracks at that time. The train was traveling from Miami to New York City. No other people were injured in the incident.
