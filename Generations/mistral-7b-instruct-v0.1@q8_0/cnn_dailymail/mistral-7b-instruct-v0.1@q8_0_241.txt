"I am pleased to say I will be available for selection on Sunday," van Persie said in an interview with MUTV. "It feels good to be back training and playing again. It's been a tough few weeks, but I have worked hard to get myself fit and ready."

Manchester United manager David Moyes will have a full squad to choose from for the match against City, with Wayne Rooney, Patrice Evra, Tom Cleverley and Marouane Fellaini all available after missing the midweek Champions League game against Real Madrid due to suspension.
