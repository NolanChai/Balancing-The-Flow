# Why Marlboro is using Justin Bieber’s name to sell cigarettes

### By . Sophie Jane Evans . and Damien Gayle . PUBLISHED: . 05:10 EST, 13 March 2014 . | . UPDATED: . 07:43 EST, 13 March 2014

The world’s largest-selling brand of cigarette is under fire for allegedly promoting smoking by using the names of pop stars like Rihanna and Justin Bieber in its packaging.

Marlboro cigarettes are sold in the UK by Philip Morris International (PMI), which has launched a new campaign in France, Spain, Italy, Switzerland, Belgium, the Netherlands
