Russian President Vladimir Putin today insisted he'll still be able to work with Mitt Romney despite the Republican presidential candidate's withdrawal from the race. Speaking at a press conference in Moscow, Putin said: 'We have always had good relations with the United States and we will continue to work together on issues of mutual interest.' He added that he was looking forward to meeting President Barack Obama in the near future, saying: 'I believe that our cooperation is important for the stability and security of the world.'
