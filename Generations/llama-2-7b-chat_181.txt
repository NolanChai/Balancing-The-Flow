Ohio girl, 5, dies after trying to escape from moving Jeep; 3-year-old friend injured. Cameron Laughlin, of Grove City, was pronounced dead at Nationwide Children's Hospital after she was pulled from the vehicle, police said. The incident occurred around 1:40 p.m. Wednesday at a park in Grove City, according to authorities. Laughlin and the three-year-old child were inside a parked car when the driver of the vehicle, who has not been identified, suddenly moved the car forward, police said. The two children were unable to escape as the vehicle accelerated, police added.
