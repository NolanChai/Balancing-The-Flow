A powerful explosion has damaged a building in the center of the Czech capital, Prague, injuring several people and causing significant damage to the surrounding area. The blast occurred at around 3am this morning, when the building was filled with dozens of people, including residents and employees. Witnesses described hearing a loud noise and seeing smoke billowing from the scene. Emergency services have rushed to the area and are working to treat the injured and search for anyone still trapped inside the building. The cause of the explosion is not yet known, but police are treating it as a criminal incident.
