2012 was a fantastic year for London with the city hosting the Olympic Games. The city was filled with excitement as people from all over the world came to watch the games and support their country. The opening ceremony was spectacular, showcasing the very best of Britain’s creativity and culture. It was also the year that the new Bond movie Skyfall was released, starring Daniel Craig as 007. This movie was highly anticipated and proved to be a huge success with audiences around the world. Finally, the ‘Gangnam Style’ craze took over the internet that year, as Psy’s catchy song and dance became an overnight sensation.
