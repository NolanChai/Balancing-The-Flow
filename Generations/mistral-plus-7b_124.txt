Television stars from Colombia have been filmed fighting with Brazilians, with plates being thrown across a restaurant as both countries count down to their quarter final clash in Fortaleza. The video emerged over the weekend and shows two men from Brazil and one from Colombia getting into an altercation at a local eatery. As the situation escalates, glasses and plates are hurled about with the three groups of friends taking part in the brawl. The incident comes ahead of the World Cup quarter final between Brazil and Columbia on Friday, with both sides desperate to reach the semi-finals.
