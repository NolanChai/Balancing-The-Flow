Sir Bob Russell MP offered a two-hour tour around his constituency town of Colchester, Essex. The hour-long drive through the centre of Colchester included visits to local landmarks such as the Castle Park, Colchester Zoo and the town’s main high street.

During the tour, Sir Bob spoke about the history and heritage of Colchester, as well as its future development plans. He emphasized the importance of preserving the town’s cultural identity while also encouraging sustainable growth and investment in the area.

Sir Bob showed a great passion for his home town throughout the tour, highlighting its natural beauty, diverse culture and vibrant community spirit.
