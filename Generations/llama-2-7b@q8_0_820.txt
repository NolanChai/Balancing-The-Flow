
A tourist family of four from the US were on a gondola ride on Sunday when they hit a local passenger boat, killing one person and injuring three more.

The crash occurred near Ponte della Pietra bridge in the northern Venice Lagoon at about 12:30 pm local time (6:30 am ET). The man who died was a worker from the lagoon who was on board the passenger boat, according to Italian news agency ANSA.

The two adults and two children were hospitalized in stable condition with head injuries. The gondola also struck another passenger vessel carrying local residents, causing damage but no injuries.
