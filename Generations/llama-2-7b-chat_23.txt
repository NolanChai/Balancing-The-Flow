Allen Sheinman, 58, never expected to fall in love again after his divorce. But then he met Emily, a 29-year-old artist who was working as a waitress at a local diner. Despite their age difference and vastly different lifestyles, the two hit it off immediately. As they spent more time together, Allen found himself falling for Emily in a way he never thought possible. He realized that love knew no boundaries, and that sometimes the most unexpected connections can lead to the greatest happiness.
