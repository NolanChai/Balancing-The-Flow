British Trader Accused of Causing $700 Billion Flash Crash on Wall Street Boasted of Making $1 Million a Week | ABC News
www.abcnews.go.com/International/wireStory/british-trader...Cached
Aug 29, 2013 · A British trader accused of triggering the $700 billion flash crash on Wall Street last year boasted he could earn £1 million in a week in "volatile" markets, according to court documents.
