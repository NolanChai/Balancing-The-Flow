(Hull Live)’  It is reported that William Kerr was arrested in relation to the murder of his ex-partner in 2015 and is currently serving a life sentence for this crime. However, he absconded from a bail hostel in Hull after being released from HMP Stockton, where he was serving his sentence. The court heard that Kerr subjected his victim to a brutal and sustained attack at her home in Hessle Road, Hull, before strangling her to death and putting her body in a cupboard at the property.
