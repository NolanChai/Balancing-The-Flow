Sharon Winters was searching for her soulmate when she met Kevin Hawke on an online dating site - but less than two weeks later she was dead after he stabbed her in a brutal knife attack. Now her brother, Danny Winters, is determined to track down the man he believes is responsible for his sister's murder and make him pay. But as Danny digs deeper, he discovers that Kevin Hawke may not be the only one with secrets - and that Sharon's murder may have been just the tip of a much darker iceberg. As he delves deeper into the case, Danny uncovers a web of lies and deceit that threatens to destroy everything he holds dear. Will he be able to uncover the truth behind his sister's tragic death before it's too late? Or will the killer succeed in getting away with murder once again?
