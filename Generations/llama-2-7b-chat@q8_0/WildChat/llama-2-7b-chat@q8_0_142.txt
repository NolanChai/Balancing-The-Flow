Here are a few ways to rephrase "Being in a doctoral program":

1. Engaged in doctoral studies: This phrase emphasizes the fact that the person is actively pursuing their degree through academic coursework and research.
2. Pursuing a doctoral degree: This phrase is similar to the previous one, but it places more focus on the actual process of earning a doctoral degree rather than simply being in a program.
3. In graduate school working towards a PhD: This phrase emphasizes that the person is enrolled in a graduate program with the ultimate goal of earning a PhD.
4. Enrolled in a doctoral program: This phrase is straightforward and to the point, conveying that the person is actively enrolled in a doctoral program and working towards completing it.
5. Doctoral candidate: This term specifically refers to someone who has advanced to the final stage of their doctoral program, typically after completing coursework and passing comprehensive exams.
