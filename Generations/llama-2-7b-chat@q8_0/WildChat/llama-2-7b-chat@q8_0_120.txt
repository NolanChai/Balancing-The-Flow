Here is a sample year-end function invitation for employees:

Dear [Employee Name],

We hope this email finds you well. As the year comes to a close, we would like to extend an invitation to our annual year-end function, which will take place on [Date and Time] at [Venue].

Please RSVP by [RSVP deadline] to let us know if you will be able to attend. If you have any dietary restrictions or special requests, please feel free to let us know so that we can accommodate you.
We look forward to seeing you there!
Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Company Name]
