Salaries in Egypt can vary based on factors such as job title, industry, location, and level of experience. Here are some approximate average monthly salaries for data science, data analyst, business analyst, cyber security, and cloud engineering positions in Egypt:
Data Science:
* Junior Data Scientist: EGP 15,000 - EGP 25,000 (around $900-$1,400 USD) per month
* Mid-level Data Scientist: EGP 30,000 - EGP 50,000 (around $1,700-$2,800 USD) per month
* Senior Data Scientist: EGP 60,000 - EGP 80,000 (around $3,400-$4,400 USD) per month
Data Analyst:
* Junior Data Analyst: EGP 12,000 - EGP 20,000 (around $700-$1,100 USD) per month
* Mid-level Data Analyst: EGP 25,000 - EGP 40,000 (around $1,400-$2,100 USD) per month
* Senior Data Analyst: EGP 50,000 - EGP 70,000 (around $2,800-$3,600 USD) per month
Business Analyst:
* Junior Business Analyst: EGP 15,000 - EGP 25,000 (around $900-$1,400 USD) per month
* Mid-level Business Analyst: EGP 30,000 - EGP 50,000 (around $1,700-$2,800 USD) per month
* Senior Business Analyst: EGP 60,000 - EGP 80,000 (around $3,400-$4,400 USD) per month
Cyber Security:
* Junior Cyber Security Specialist: EGP 15,000 - EGP 25,000 (around $900-$1,400 USD) per month
* Mid-level Cyber Security Analyst: EGP 30,000 - EGP 50,000 (around $1,700-$2,800 USD) per month
* Senior Cyber Security Specialist: EGP 60,000 - EGP 80,000 (around $3,400-$4,400 USD) per month
Cloud Engineering:
* Junior Cloud Engineer: EGP 15,000 - EGP 25,000 (around $900-$1,400 USD) per month
* Mid-level Cloud Engineer: EGP 30,000 - EGP 50,000 (around $1,700-$2,800 USD) per month
* Senior Cloud Engineer: EGP 60,000 - EGP 80,000 (around $3,400-$4,400 USD) per month
It's worth noting that these figures are approximate and may vary based on factors such as the specific industry, company size, and location within Egypt. Additionally, salaries can vary depending on the candidate's level of experience, education, and certifications.
