
This is a report about the sexual violence that has been going on in this small orthodox community for years. The reporter Becky Evans tries to talk to the victims and their families but they don’t want to speak out as they feel ashamed of the incident. She also interviews a victim who doesn’t mind talking about the incident she went through and that is the reason she is called ‘The Brave One’. The reporter talks to the police as well and finds out that no action has been taken against the perpetrators so far. Becky Evans tries to find out why these incidents are occurring in this small orthodox community but is unable to come up with any reasonable explanation.

### Keywords:

1. Rape
2. Sexual assault
3. Isolation
4. Orthodox community
5. Police inaction
6. No action against perpetrators
