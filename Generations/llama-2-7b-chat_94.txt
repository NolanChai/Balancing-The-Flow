France's 5-2 thrashing of Sweden in the World Cup quarterfinals was met with a mixture of shock and admiration from fans around the globe, but especially from their English counterparts who had been relying on Les Bleus to provide a much-needed morale boost. With this stunning victory, France has cemented its place as one of the top contenders for the tournament title, leaving England in a state of disarray and raising questions about their own chances of advancing any further.
