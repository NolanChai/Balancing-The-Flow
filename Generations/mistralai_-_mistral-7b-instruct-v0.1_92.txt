 Detective Chief Inspector Mark Manterfield said police were called to Tallow Beach at around 7am by concerned members of the public who had found the body of a woman in the sand dunes near the beach.

"Police are working with forensic experts to determine the cause of death and identify the woman," Mr Manterfield said.

"At this stage, it is unknown how she came to be in the dunes or why she was there."

The woman's body has been taken to the morgue for further examination.
