Charlie Sheen has announced that he will be returning to work on the hit television show "Two and a Half Men" later this month. Sheen recently completed a rehabilitation program for substance abuse and is eager to return to the set of the popular sitcom, which he has starred in since 2003. The actor's representative has confirmed that Sheen will be back on set by February 28th, after a three-month hiatus from his role as Charlie Harper.
