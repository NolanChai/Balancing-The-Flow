The mysterious disappearance of the Mona Lisa's eyebrows and eyelashes is a perplexing art historical mystery. Leonardo da Vinci, the artist who painted the Mona Lisa, likely removed the brows and lashes during the painting process for artistic reasons. The removal of her eyebrows and eyelashes adds to the enigmatic nature of the Mona Lisa's expression and further accentuates her piercing gaze. While the true reason behind their disappearance remains a mystery, it has certainly added an element of mystique to one of the most famous works of art in history.
