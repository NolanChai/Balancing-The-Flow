The crew left Port Clyde on Sunday afternoon, bound for Boothbay Harbor and Portland, Maine, but were forced to seek shelter due to heavy seas in Boothbay Harbor. The schooner's skipper contacted Coast Guard Sector Northern New England at 12:07 p.m. Monday, requesting assistance due to rough weather conditions and loss of propulsion.

The crew of the MLB located the stricken vessel, which was anchored off Boothbay Harbor, at 3:46 p.m. The 47-footer brought them aboard and transported them back to South Portland around 5 p.m.
