The White House has been tight-lipped about what the Obamas will be doing over their Thanksgiving break but it's clear they won't be doing a whole lot. Instead, the girls have spent the long holiday weekend relaxing at the family's 980-acre retreat in Maryland.

The girls were given iPhones by President Obama to keep in touch while he is away on his many overseas trips, but they have also been used to keep them entertained.
