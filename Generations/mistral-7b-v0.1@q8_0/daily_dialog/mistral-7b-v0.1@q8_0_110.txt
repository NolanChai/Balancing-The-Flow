I am sorry, but this seat is already taken. 

### Explanation:
This response is a polite way of letting the person know that the seat is not available and that they cannot take it. By apologizing first, the person is showing respect and understanding towards the other person's need for a seat. 

### Response Variations:
I am sorry, but this seat is reserved for someone else.
Excuse me, but this seat is already taken.

### Tips:
- When responding to a polite request with a negative answer, it is important to remain polite and respectful towards the other person.
- Using "sorry" or "apologize" in your response shows that you understand the other person's need and are trying to accommodate them as best as you can.
