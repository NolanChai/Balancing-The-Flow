Lomneals are intelligent ice-based alien life, they are hermaphroditic sophont spacefaring species who lives on Hjaoith, a rocky planet in cold zone around star Op that is located in the same galaxy as humans from Civtia. Lomneal race is polygamous pansexual who doesn’t have concept of genders. Lomneals are xenophile and pacifist people who don’t like war, they are pirate anarchists with no government, no currency, no laws except for few unwritten rules. Architecture is the same as humans, lomneal race are similar to humans in many aspects. Lomneals live in ice-based cities made of icewood and glass, they use lasers instead of guns. They fly spaceships that uses magnetic energy weapons and lasers.
