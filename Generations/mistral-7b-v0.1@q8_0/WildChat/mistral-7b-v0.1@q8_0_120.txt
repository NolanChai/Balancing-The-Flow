Instructions: This letter can be used when you want to send an invitation for your company's year-end party to all your employees. Make sure you send this out in enough time so they can mark their calendars and plan accordingly. It is a good idea to send out the invitations as soon as possible once you have decided on the date, location and details of the event.

Dear [Name],

You are cordially invited to join us for our company's end of year party that will be held at [Venue] on [Date].

[Event starts: Time]

[Event ends: time]

Kindly RSVP to this email or by calling [your number/employee name and number], so we can plan the appropriate food and drinks for all those attending. We look forward to seeing you there!
