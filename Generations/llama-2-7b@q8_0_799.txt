+ . 02. It was also ordered that Google must give customers the option of opting out of such advertising practices without losing access to their other services.

### Analysis:

+ . 01. The watchdog's decision means French users will be given more information about how they are targeted with adverts online. 
+ . 02. This is because Google, like many others, collects data on people to serve them more relevant advertising and track their internet use for market research purposes.
