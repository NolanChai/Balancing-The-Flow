The tiny, blonde ballerina - who has been comforting Sir Mick Jagger after the death of his long-term girlfriend L'Wren Scott - was spotted wandering the streets on Monday night looking glum.

She was clad in a black dress and sweater as she left the couple's London home - the property is now said to be under 24 hour armed guard - carrying two bags filled with personal belongings.
