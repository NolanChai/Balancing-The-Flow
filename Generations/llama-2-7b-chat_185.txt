Alicia Gray, a 23-year-old woman from Kentucky, has pleaded guilty to two counts of second-degree sodomy and one count of third-degree sodomy. According to the police report, Gray had sexual contact with a 14-year-old boy on multiple occasions, including once in her home and another time in a parked car. The incidents occurred between July 2013 and September 2013, and Gray was arrested in October of that year. Gray is currently being held at the Boyd County Detention Center on a $50,000 cash bond.
