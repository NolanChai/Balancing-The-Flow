Rick Ross, the popular and controversial Miami-based rapper known for his extravagant lifestyle and tough talk, crashed his silver Rolls Royce into an apartment building after hearing gunfire along a Fort Lauderdale, Florida, street Monday morning, according to police.

The incident occurred around 4:30 a.m., when police received a call about shots fired in the area of Southwest 12th Avenue and Second Street. Officers responded to the scene and discovered that an occupied apartment building had been hit by gunfire. They then located the Rolls Royce, which had crashed into the building.

The driver of the car was identified as Ross, who was taken to a local hospital for treatment of minor injuries. No other injuries were reported in connection with the incident.

Ross has not yet commented on the crash.
