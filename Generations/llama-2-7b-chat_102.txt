As the bus was driving through the small town of Wagga Wagga in New South Wales, Australia, a group of locals suddenly began taunting and intimidating a group of French tourists on board. The incident occurred on Wednesday evening, leaving the French visitors visibly shaken and frightened. According to eyewitnesses, the locals hurled insults at the tourists, even threatening to kill them. The bus driver was allegedly unable to intervene, as he was also subjected to abuse by the mob. The incident has sparked outrage among local residents and authorities are now investigating the matter.
