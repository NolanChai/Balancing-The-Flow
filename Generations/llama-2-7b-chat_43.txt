Kevin Pietersen’s autobiography, “From Boundary to Boundary”, is a tell-all account of his life in cricket, revealing the inner workings of the English dressing room and the vulnerabilities of elite athletes. In the book, he shares intimate details of his struggles with mental health, the pressures of playing for England, and the complex dynamics of team dynamics. With unflinching honesty, Pietersen pulls back the curtain on the high-stakes world of professional sport, exposing the very human frailties that lie beneath the surface of athletic success.
