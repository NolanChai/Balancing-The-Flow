He's fighting for his life - literally! Manny Pacquiao says he has to fight Floyd Mayweather like it is the last of his life when he touches gloves with the undefeated American in the $300m bout on May 2. The Filipino, who is 48-5-2 and 38KOs, has been installed as 1/17 favourite to beat 'Money' but believes a loss could have long lasting consequences for his future career.
