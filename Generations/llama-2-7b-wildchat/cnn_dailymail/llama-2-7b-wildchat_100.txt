Stephanie Scott's accused killer Vincent Stanford has been charged with her murder - as his family issue a statement about the teenage mother-of-one. Stephanie Scott, 38, was found dead at a house in Leeton last Sunday after allegedly being killed by Stanford. Her accused killer, who is believed to be the father of Ms Scott's child, has been charged with her murder and will front court this week. Now his family have issued a statement about his arrest on Wednesday. 

"Vincent and our families are deeply saddened by what has occurred," they said in the statement, released to Daily Mail Australia. "Our thoughts are with Stephanie's family who lost their loved one too soon." The Scott and Stanford families have asked for privacy as they grieve over the death of Ms Scott.
