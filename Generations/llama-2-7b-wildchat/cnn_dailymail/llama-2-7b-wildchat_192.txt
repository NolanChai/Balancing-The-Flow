An artist has carved out a unique genre for herself with her colourful portraits of celebrity bottoms. Spanish artist Catalina Viejo, 31, who is based in New York, paints miniature pictures from candy wrappers using a technique called encaustic, which involves heating wax and pigments together to create translucent paint. Her series, ‘Little Bottoms’, consists of tiny portraits of celebrity bums on the backs of Jelly Babies and Skittles candies, with each piece taking up just a fraction of an inch. The artist uses a technique called encaustic to create translucent paintings
