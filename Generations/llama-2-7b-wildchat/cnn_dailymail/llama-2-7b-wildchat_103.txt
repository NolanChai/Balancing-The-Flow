### Explanation:
Diana was widely regarded as a symbol of hope for people suffering with AIDS during the 80s, visiting patients in hospitals and charity events. She also visited the bereaved families of those who had been killed by an IRA bomb at Wembley stadium on May 21st 1985. In 1997, a memorial garden was opened to commemorate the victims at the site of the bombing, which Diana laid the first rose.
