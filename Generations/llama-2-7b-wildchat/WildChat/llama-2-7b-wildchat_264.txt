It's possible for mothers to have Narcissistic Personality Disorder, but they usually manifest with less of the grandiose and exploitative behaviors that are more commonly associated with male narcissists. Instead, moms who are NPD tend to be highly sensitive to criticism and may struggle to emotionally detach from their children. They also tend to have high expectations for their kids' achievements but may not be able to provide the kind of unconditional support that healthy mothers would give.
