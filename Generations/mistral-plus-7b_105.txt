With their showstopping Olympic appearance and glitzy new West End musical, the Spice Girls are back in the limelight.

The girl band first shot to fame as part of a media frenzy which surrounded their debut single Wannabe, released 19 years ago today. The song’s message of girl power struck a chord with many young women and cemented the group’s place in pop culture history.

Since then, each member has gone on to have their own solo careers as well as enjoying family life. Here is what they are doing now:

Victoria Beckham

Victoria, also known as ‘Posh Spice’, is now a successful fashion designer and lives in West London with her husband David Beckham and three children. She recently launched her own range of sunglasses and is reportedly worth £270 million.
