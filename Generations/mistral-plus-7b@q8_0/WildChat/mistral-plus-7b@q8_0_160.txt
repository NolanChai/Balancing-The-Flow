This is the mindset all book version. It’s a 12-week program to help you create the habits and systems needed for success. The goal is simple: develop a growth mindset, which will allow you to improve yourself every day. This includes things like developing confidence in your abilities, setting achievable goals, building resilience against failure, and getting rid of self-doubt. You’ll be guided through this process with daily lessons that teach important skills related to improving your mindset. The program also includes exercises designed to help you develop these new habits and systems faster than ever before!
