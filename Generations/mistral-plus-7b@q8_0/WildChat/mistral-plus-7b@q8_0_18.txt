In the 2007 VPI Football Season, the Hokies had an overall record of 11-3. This was led by the leadership and coaching of Frank Beamer. The season began with a 49-17 victory over East Carolina University. After that they were victorious against Virginia Military Institute (26-3), Troy University (58-10), Wake Forest (34-10) and Boston College (26-3). However, on November 17th they lost their first game of the season to Georgia Tech (28-7) and ended up losing another game in December to West Virginia University for the Gator Bowl.

The 2007 VPI Football Team was a success. With a record of 11-3, they won a lot of games and only lost a couple. The team was coached by Frank Beamer who did an amazing job leading the Hokies to victory in most of their games.
