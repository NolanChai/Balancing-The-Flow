Materials:

- Potassium Permanganate (KMnO4) 3 grams
- Sugar 5 Grams
- Water 30ml
- Acetone 15ml
- Funnel
- Large Bowl
- Small container with lid

Instructions:

1. Measure out the Potassium Permanganate (KMnO4) and sugar into a large bowl.
2. Pour in the water and acetone. Stir well to dissolve the ingredients.
3. Pour the mixture into a small container with a lid.
4. To use, light the mixture and toss onto the ground or into the air.
