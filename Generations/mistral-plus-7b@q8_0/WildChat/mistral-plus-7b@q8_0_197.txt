Jamey Chadwell Stadium is a college football stadium located in Conway, South Carolina. It serves as the home of the Coastal Carolina Chanticleers football team. The stadium opened on September 5, 2015 and seats 3,000 people.

The stadium was built at a cost of $80 million and features an artificial turf field, a video scoreboard, and a press box. Jamey Chadwell Stadium is the first major collegiate sports venue in Conway and has helped to boost tourism and economic development in the area.
