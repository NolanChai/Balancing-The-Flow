- Name: Red Flower with white stripes
- Type: Dahlia (dah-LYE-uh)
- Color: red
- Zone: 5 to 9
- Height: 24″ – 36″
- Exposure: Sun to Part Sun
- Bloom Time: Mid Summer
- Soil Moisture: Moist well drained soil
- Planting Instructions: plant tubers in the spring after the last frost. Dig a hole about 4” deep and place the tuber so the eyes are facing up and the roots down. Fill in with soil and water thoroughly.
