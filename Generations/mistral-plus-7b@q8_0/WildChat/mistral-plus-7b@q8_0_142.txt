Being in a doctoral program is an excellent way to learn about the research process and to develop your own ideas for research. A doctoral program typically involves several years of coursework, as well as a thesis or dissertation, which you will write based on your original research. The coursework in a doctoral program usually includes advanced classes in your field of study, as well as seminars and research methods classes. In addition to the coursework, a doctoral program also includes comprehensive exams, qualifying exams, and finally the dissertation itself.
