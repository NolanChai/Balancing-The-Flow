## Basic Email Etiquette

When it comes to email etiquette, there are a few simple rules to follow:

- Use a professional tone in your emails. Avoid slang, colloquialisms, or emoticons that might come across as unprofessional.
- Use proper grammar and punctuation in your messages. This will help convey a sense of professionalism and ensure that your message is easily understood.
- Be courteous and respectful in all of your communications. Take the time to thank people for their assistance, acknowledge their contributions, and show appreciation for their efforts.
