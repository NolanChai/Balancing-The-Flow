Job Role Qualifications

A quality engineer has to have a bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering, mechanical engineering or electronics and communication engineering. A master’s degree will add to the credibility of an applicant for this position. It is preferred if you possess relevant work experience in the field along with a professional certification from an accredited body.

Qualities required

A quality engineer should have a logical mindset and be thorough by nature. They must pay attention to detail and also possess excellent analytical skills. They need good communication skills and should be willing to learn new things on a daily basis. They should be creative thinkers as well.
