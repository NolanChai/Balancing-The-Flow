All of these Demon Legion Commanders have their own unique abilities and playstyle, each with their strengths and weaknesses. Valtan is known for his powerful strikes and unpredictable moves, while Brelshaza is famous for her quick and agile attacks. Kakul Saydon is a master of confusion and trickery, using mind-bending magic to confuse his opponents. Akkan is deadly with poisonous attacks and thrives on chaos, while Thaemine commands the shadows with his terrifying dark powers.
