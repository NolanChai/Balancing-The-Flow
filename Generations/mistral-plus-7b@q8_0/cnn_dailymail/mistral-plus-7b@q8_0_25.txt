The Foreign Office has come under fire for its handling of his case, with critics claiming it was not doing enough to help Phil. His mother Carolyn told the Daily Mail that her son had been ‘abandoned’ by the Foreign Office and demanded to know why he wasn’t receiving more support. She said: “The Foreign Office is doing nothing for my son. They have abandoned him.” The Foreign Office said it was providing consular assistance to Phil, including ensuring his welfare and liaising with local authorities. A spokesman added that its staff were working on the case ‘in accordance with our published guidance’.
