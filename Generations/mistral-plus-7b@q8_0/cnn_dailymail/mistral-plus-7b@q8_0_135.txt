Once an order is placed, the driver picks up the product from the shop and delivers it to the customer within half an hour of placing the request. The company boasts a 30-minute average delivery time, with no minimum order size. Eaze is available in California for those aged over 18, who can place orders ranging from joints and pre-rolled cones to edibles, oils and other concentrates. Users are required to submit proof of age, photo ID, address and medical card to sign up for an account. The company plans to expand into Oregon and Washington later this year, and in Colorado during 2016.
