The passengers were not injured during the crash landing, but around 100 of them had to stay on board while emergency services dealt with the situation. They were allowed off the plane after an hour and taken back to the terminal by bus.

An investigation into what caused the incident has been launched by the Air Accidents Investigation Branch. The plane was operated by Ryanair and carrying 167 passengers from Faro in Portugal to East Midlands Airport.
