The Ministry of Defence said it was "not possible" to say how many of the women were forced to have abortions, but figures obtained by the Press Association showed that 25 servicewomen and their dependents had been flown off ships from 1996 to April this year. In a third of cases the reason for the medevac was given as pregnancy. HMS Ocean is one of the Royal Navy's largest warships, with an all-female medical unit on board. It has seen action in Iraq and the Balkans and regularly serves on counter piracy patrols off the Horn of Africa. The MoD said that during its latest tour of duty to the Gulf in 2013, a pregnant servicewoman was flown off for treatment and gave birth while under military care.
