However, the club instead opted for Julen Lopetegui as manager and Carlos has now admitted he would prefer to take charge of Brazil in the future rather than re-join his former side. "I have always said that I want to be a coach," he told FourFourTwo. "My dream is to coach the Brazil national team.

"I really like working with young players and developing them, helping them grow into great footballers. It's very easy for me as I had so many experiences in my career. As a player, it was much more difficult. When I became a coach I started to enjoy it very much."
