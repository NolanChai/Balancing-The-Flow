It’s a spirit that has been simmering below the surface since at least November 2008 — one that I sensed even during my own election campaign last fall. It is a spirit of defiance, hope and, most importantly, unity. The people who are fueling this revolution come from every background: young and old; white, black, Asian, Latino, Native American; Christian, Jew, Muslim, non-believer. They share a desire for change.
