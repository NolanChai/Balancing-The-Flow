They have been granted permission by the British Consulate in Paris, which is now arranging the repatriation of the 15-year-old from Chalet Mont Blanc in Megeve.

Carwyn, of Clifford Street, St Helier, was holidaying in France with his mother Julie and stepfather Richard. He suffered a severe head injury when he fell while skiing at 1pm on Thursday. Paramedics battled for an hour to save the teenager, but he never regained consciousness.

Julie said: 'It's been such a terrible shock for everyone who knew him. Carwyn was such a caring and loving young man.'

Mr Scott-Howell added: 'He will be sadly missed by all his family and friends.'
