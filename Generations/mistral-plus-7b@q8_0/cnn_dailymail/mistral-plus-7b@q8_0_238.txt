The 49-year-old free climber used his bare hands and feet to scale the building, which is officially known as the Infinity Tower. He took three hours to reach the top of the tower that curves at a 90 degree angle.

It’s not Robert's first record breaking climb. He’s been scaling buildings for decades and has previously scaled the world’s tallest building in Taiwan, the Petronas Towers in Malaysia, and even the Burj Khalifa in Dubai.
