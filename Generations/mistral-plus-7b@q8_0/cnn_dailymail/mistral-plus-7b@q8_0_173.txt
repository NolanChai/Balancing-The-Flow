They are one of the most feared animals on the planet. Now fascinating images reveal how fish make a collective effort to stay out of the way of sharks. The striking photographs show a shoal of fish mingling together, as if they are trying to avoid the attention of predators lurking nearby.

In the pictures taken by wildlife photographer Michael Muller in California, hundreds of tiny fish seem to huddle close together for protection. Scientists have long believed that schools of fish form a collective defence against sharks and other predators. But the latest research has shown this behaviour is far more sophisticated than originally thought.
