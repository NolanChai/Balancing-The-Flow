Yes, I know you're all grown up now. But still it doesn't hurt to be reminded of some safety tips when riding a bicycle. Here are some tips for cycling safely:

- Wear a helmet every time you ride your bike.
- Obey the rules of the road.
- Be predictable and make sure drivers see you.
- Ride with traffic, not against it.
- Use hand signals to let others know what you're going to do.
