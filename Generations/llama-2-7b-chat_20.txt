Sir Bob Russell MP offered a two-hour tour around his constituency town of Colchester. The Labour MP has been representing the town since 2005 and said he was "delighted" to show visitors around. He claimed that Colchester had a lot to offer, including a rich history dating back to Roman times and a vibrant cultural scene. During the tour, Sir Bob highlighted some of the town's key landmarks, including the famous Colchester Castle, which dates back to the 12th century. He also pointed out the town's historic High Street, which is lined with picturesque timber-framed buildings and home to many independent shops and cafes.
