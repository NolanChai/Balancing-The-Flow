 The accident occurred on Interstate 70 eastbound near Indianapolis around 1:30 p.m., the Indiana State Police said.

The chain reaction began with a car crash involving three vehicles in the left lane of the highway. When one of the cars caught fire, it caused more drivers to swerve and collide, setting off a series of crashes that involved as many as 40 vehicles.

Those injured in the accident were taken to nearby hospitals for treatment. The extent of their injuries is unknown.

The cause of the crash remains under investigation.
