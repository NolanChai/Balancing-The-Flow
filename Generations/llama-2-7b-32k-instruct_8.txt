
Pensioner dies after being hit in the head by cricket ball during game (From Mail Online)

The victim, who has not been named, suffered serious head injury when he was struck in the face with the ball at Whiteley Bank recreational ground on Sunday morning. He died later that day in hospital. The incident happened as 20 people watched on after a match between local sides Twickenham Green and Richmond Rams . A spokesperson for Richmond Council said: "This is clearly an extremely unfortunate accident, but the pitch is not managed by us and we are not aware of any previous incidents like this one."
