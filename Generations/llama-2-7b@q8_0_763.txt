
- There is no need to use 'over-zealous'. You can directly say that the transport chief has put up too many parking signs.
- Use 'accused of' instead of 'been accused of'

```ruby
  # Good
  - Over-zealous transport chiefs have been accused of wasting taxpayers’ money after putting up 63 ‘no parking’ signs along a one-mile country lane.

  # Bad
  - Over-zealous transport chiefs have been accusing of wasting taxpayers’ money after putting up 63 'no parking' signs along a one-mile country lane.
```
