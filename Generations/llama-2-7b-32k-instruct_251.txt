Arrest made in Mexico's murderous drug war (usatoday.com)USATODAY.COM - Mexican authorities announced the arrest Thursday of one of the country's most-wanted drug cartel bosses, the man widely known as "El Chapo" or Shorty who has been accused in recent weeks of ordering more than 30 killings in his home state of Tamaulipas. The suspect, Joaquin "El Chapo" Guzmn, is a former policeman who turned to crime and rose quickly through the ranks of the Gulf Cartel as it battled the Zetas for control of the region.
