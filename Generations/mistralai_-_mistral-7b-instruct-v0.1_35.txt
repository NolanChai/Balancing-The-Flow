 The article is about a series of night-time rapes that took place in an isolated, orthodox Christian community. The rapes went on for several months and left the victims traumatized. Despite the efforts of local authorities to apprehend the perpetrator, no progress was made until a brave witness came forward with information that led to the arrest of the rapist. The article also highlights the challenges faced by the community in coming to terms with the crimes committed in their midst and the efforts being made to support the victims and prevent similar incidents from happening in the future.
